Delicious beef tacos

Prepare beef tacous easily with a few ingredients and in a short time

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • 200 gr of beef steak
  • 1 spoon of vegetable oil
  • 2 spoons of powdered chicken broth
  • Onion
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop beef steak into small pieces.(About 1 inch squares)
  2. Chop about 1/8 of an onion into small pieces
  3. Add the oil to a pan and set stove heat to medium
  4. Add the onion and let it cook for a minute
  5. Add the beef to the pan
  6. Add 2 spoons of powdered chicken broth
  7. Add some pepper
  8. Mix everything to make sure it is cooking evenly
  9. Heat some tortillas
  10. Chop some Coriander leaves
  11. Place the tortillas in a plate and once the beef is well cooked remove from fire and place over the tortillas
  12. Add the Coriander leaves on top of the beef in the taco
  13. For the best flavour add lime and salt
