Tomato Cream Spaghuetti

Tasty and creamy tomato Spaghetti

Easy and delicious to prepare and ready to serve in 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 250 grams of Spaghetti
  • Half a liter of water
  • A spoon of salt
  • 25 grams of butter
  • 210ml of tomato puree
  • 100ml of cream
  • 10 grams of oregano
  • A spoon of powdered chicken broth
  • 50 grams of sliced gouda cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the water and add the Salt
  2. Once the water is boling add the spaghetti or pasta of your choice
  3. While the pasta is cooking add the butter to a pan heating on the stove for it to melt
  4. Once molten, add the tomato puree and mix. Heat until the tomato puree bubbles
  5. When the tomato puree is covered in bubbles add the cream
  6. Mix thoroughly
  7. Lower the heat and add the oregano and powdered chicken broth
  8. Mix and once it is all together add the pasta and mix it
  9. Finally add some gouda cheese and let it melt
  10. Serve the pasta on a bowl and add parmesan cheese as desired
