Eggs with chorizo

Delicious eggs with chorizo for a great breakfast

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 200 grams of chorizo
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Break the three eggs and pour them into a bowl to scramble them
  2. Add salt and pepper to the mixed eggs
  3. Heat the pan and place the chorizo on it
  4. Once the outside of the chorizo is cooked make it into small pieces with a spatula
  5. Let the chorizo cook until it has a darker color, without getting black
  6. Once the chorizo is cooked pour the eggs over it and let it cook
  7. Scramble the eggs with chorizo to make sure it is well cooked and the serve it on a plate
  8. Enjoy!
